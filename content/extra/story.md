I was born in late 90s in Amritsar, Punjab. Growing up with a father who worked as a software engineer, I developed an early interest in technology. At 11 years old, I built a Railway Ticket Booking System using Visual Basic. 

## Education
During school, I focused on Mathematics, eventually scoring 99/100 in my final exams. Because I enjoyed the subject so much, I initially wanted to become a mathematician. However, following my father's advice to choose a path with better job prospects, I transitioned into Computer Science Engineering instead. In 2016, I enrolled at Guru Nanak Dev University (GNDU). Two months into the program, I began learning Python, which deepened my interest in programming.

## Early Experience and Google Summer of Code
During my third year of university, I participated in the Google Summer of Code with the GNOME Foundation, where I updated the [GTK website](https://gtk.org). Based on this work, I was invited to speak at a conference in Greece, though my visa application was ultimately denied. Following this project, I began freelancing as a programmer and technical writer.

## Post-Graduate Studies and Entrepreneurship
After earning my bachelor's degree, I chose to pursue a Master's degree in Computer Science, also at GNDU. During the academic delays caused by the COVID-19 pandemic, I co-founded [RavSam](https://www.ravsam.in), a digital product agency that develops full-stack applications and produces technical content for businesses.

## Professional Career
In early 2022, as I was completing my post-graduate studies, the company I had been freelancing for offered me a full-time position as a Software Engineer. I accepted the role directly, bypassing traditional job portals and cover letter submissions.
